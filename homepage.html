About

Some History About us

  • Early 2022

    Our Humble Beginnings

    USAAS was founded to address the unique challenges faced by international students during their transition to a new academic environment. The system was envisioned as a one-stop platform to simplify registration, provide transportation, and offer translation assistance

  • March 2023

    An Agency is Born

    In 2023, few China Student and I at UTM recognized the need for a platform dedicated to assisting international students.

  • December 2023

    Transition to Full Service

    Since its launch, USAAS has grown rapidly, assisting over 200+ international students every semester. Key features such as e-hailing for transportation, in-app communication, and real-time updates were added, making it an indispensable tool for students."

  • July 2024

    Phase Two Expansion

    As USAAS continues to grow, its vision is to expand its services to other universities and provide comprehensive support for students worldwide. The goal remains to create a global network of assistance and support for students wherever they go.
